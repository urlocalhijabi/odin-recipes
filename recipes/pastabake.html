Pasta Bake

Description

A comforting and hearty dish, not to mention a good way to add vegetables in a way you'll actually enjoy! It's an oven dish that can be made in under 40 minutes. Here's what you'll need:

  • Ingredients:
  • 400g dried pasta
  • An onion
  • A bell pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 800g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 100g baby spinach
  • 100g cheddar
  • 100g mozzarella
  • Broccoli
  • Cauliflower
  • Salt and pepper - season to taste
  • Instructions:
  • Preheat the oven to 190C
  • Boil a large pan of slightly salted water and cook the pasta for 10 minutes. Drain
  • Chop the bell pepper, garlic cloves and onion
  • Heat a pot and put tinned tomatoes,garlic cloves, onion and salt & pepper on a low heat.
  • Boil broccoli and cauliflower until cooked
  • Once all done put pasta, veg and sauce in one big glass dish and sprinkle cheeses on top
  • Bake in oven for 10-15 mins and you're done!
