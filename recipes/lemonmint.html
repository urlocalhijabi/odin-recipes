Lemon Mint

Description:

A versatile and savoury dish that provides lots of protein, veg (and lots of cheese.) Easy to make and can be cooked in under 10 minutes. Here's what you'll need:

  • Ingredients:
  • Mint
  • Lemons
  • Sugar
  • Ice (optional)
  • Equipment: a juicer or a blender of some kind
  • Instructions:
  • Chop up your mint into smaller pieces
  • Squeeze lemons (or just peel them if u have a juicer) until it reaches 1/4 of the serving container
  • Blend the two, ensuring that you include ice to make it extra cold
  • Put the blended liquid through a strainer then add cold water
