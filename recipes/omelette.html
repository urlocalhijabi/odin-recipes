Omelette

Description:

A versatile and savoury dish that provides lots of protein, veg (and lots of cheese.) Easy to make and can be cooked in under 10 minutes. Here's what you'll need:

  • Ingredients:
  • 3 large eggs
  • Spring onion
  • A bell pepper
  • Spinach
  • Fetta
  • Chedder or red leister
  • Mozzarella
  • Ps: I didn't include measurments as you can put any amount of the other items as you like!
  • Salt and pepper - season to taste
  • Instructions:
  • Preheat the pan and add some spray butter
  • Crack and whisk eggs into a bowl, then add salt and black pepper.
  • Wash and chop a sufficient amount of spring onion, spinach and bell pepper.
  • Put all chopped items into pan and allow them to sizzle for a minute.
  • Add egg mixture into pan
  • Get your cheese and distribute around the pan
  • Cook until each side is a golden colour and you are done!
